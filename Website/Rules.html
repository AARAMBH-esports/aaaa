Rules

1. Eligibility & Registration

Players must be at least level 15, and their in-game ID must match the registered ID.

All participants must register before the tournament starts, and registration will close 24 hours before the event.

2. Payment

You must send your payment screenshot, and ensure the details are correct. Payments should be made via our specified methods.

3. Fair Play

No hacking is allowed. Any players found cheating or using unauthorized software will be disqualified immediately.

4. Match ID Usage

Only the registered ID can be used. If you need to change it, notify us at least 45 hours before the match.

After the match, you must submit a screenshot of your match results and eliminations.
